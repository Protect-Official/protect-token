Step by Step

1. Automatic Fee Collection

Every time $PROTECT tokens move, the Token-2022 protocol withholds 1.5%. This happens automatically ‚Äî no manual intervention, no way to bypass it.

2. Monthly Fee Harvesting

On the 1st of each month, accumulated fees are collected from all token accounts into one place. This transaction is public and verifiable.

3. Three-Way Split

Fees are distributed to three separate wallets ‚Äî donation, operations, and LP. All wallet addresses are public.

FAQ

Can the team steal the donation funds?
All wallet addresses are public and every transaction is on-chain. We're implementing multi-signature wallets (Squads Protocol) that require multiple approvals for any transfer. You can watch our wallets 24/7.
What if an NGO refuses the donation?
Funds get redirected to the remaining partner NGOs or a community-voted alternative.
Can the 1.5% fee be changed?
The TransferFee is set when the token is created. We plan to renounce the fee authority after launch, making it permanent and unchangeable ‚Äî forever.
How do I know the donations actually happen?
Every donation is documented with: on-chain transaction hash, NGO receipt, and a public report. Don't trust us ‚Äî verify it yourself.
