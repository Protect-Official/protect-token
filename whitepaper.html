$PROTECT Whitepaper

Version 2.0 ‚Äî February 2026

Protecting the Innocent. On-Chain.

Abstract

$PROTECT is a Solana-based community token with a built-in donation mechanism dedicated to fighting child exploitation worldwide. Every transaction contributes directly to verified NGOs through transparent, on-chain donations.

Built on Token-2022 with native TransferFee ‚Äî no external contracts, no workarounds. The fee is enforced at the protocol level.

"The files are open. Now we act."

1. Problem

Child exploitation and trafficking remain among the most underreported and underfunded crises globally. Despite high-profile revelations, systemic action remains slow. Meanwhile, the crypto space generates billions in daily volume ‚Äî yet rarely channels that energy toward meaningful causes.

2. Solution

  • Meme energy drives attention and community
  • Built-in transfer fee ensures consistent funding (protocol-level)
  • On-chain transparency proves every dollar donated
  • Community governance lets holders choose where funds go
  • Long-term vision: Building blockchain infrastructure for child protection

Phase 1 ‚Äî Manual (Launch ~ 3 months): Fees accumulate in public donation wallet. Monthly transfers to NGOs. Transaction hashes shared publicly. NGO receipts published.
