Protecting the Innocent. On-Chain.

Abstract

$PROTECT is a Solana-based community token with a built-in donation mechanism dedicated to fighting child exploitation worldwide. Every transaction contributes directly to verified NGOs through transparent, on-chain donations.

Built on Token-2022 with native TransferFee ‚Äî no external contracts, no workarounds. The fee is enforced at the protocol level.

"The files are open. Now we act."

1. Problem

Child exploitation and trafficking remain among the most underreported and underfunded crises globally. Despite high-profile revelations, systemic action remains slow. Meanwhile, the crypto space generates billions in daily volume ‚Äî yet rarely channels that energy toward meaningful causes.

2. Solution

  • Meme energy drives attention and community
  • Built-in transfer fee ensures consistent funding (protocol-level)
  • On-chain transparency proves every dollar donated
  • Community governance lets holders choose where funds go
  • Long-term vision: Building blockchain infrastructure for child protection

8. Anti-Bot Protection System

$PROTECT implements a comprehensive multi-layer anti-bot and anti-whale mechanism to ensure fair distribution and protect against malicious actors.

On-Chain Protection (Token-2022 Transfer Hook)

  • Launch Period Protection: First 10 minutes after launch ‚Äî max 1M tokens per transaction
  • Maximum Wallet Limits: Protocol-level enforcement prevents single wallets from accumulating excessive amounts
  • Transaction Cooldowns: Time-based limits between consecutive transactions from the same wallet
  • MEV Protection: Integration with Jupiter's MEV protection features

Off-Chain Monitoring System ‚úÖ Active

Real-time bot detection system operational: Advanced pattern recognition monitors all transactions for suspicious behavior including consecutive trading, launch sniping, and wallet clustering.

Risk Scoring: 0-100 scale with automatic alerts at threshold violations. Auto-blacklist functionality for confirmed bot wallets.

Performance: <100ms detection latency, 0% false positive rate in testing scenarios.
